# User Personas

**Persona 1: Alex (Primary User)**
- **Age:** 22
- **Occupation:** University Student
- **Behavior:** Alex owns over 100 books and 50 movies. They frequently buy second-hand books and often forget if they already own a specific title. They lend textbooks to friends and lose track.
- **Goal:** Want to quickly check if they own a book before buying it and track who has their books.
- **Tech Level:** Comfortable using web apps.

**Persona 2: Maria (Secondary User)**
- **Age:** 35
- **Occupation:** Marketing Manager
- **Behavior:** Maria loves movies and subscribes to multiple streaming platforms. She forgets which movies she has already watched and often re-watches things by accident.
- **Goal:** Want to mark watched movies and keep a "Favorites" list.
- **Tech Level:** Basic tech skills.
