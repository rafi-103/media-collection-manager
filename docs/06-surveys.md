# Surveys

A small survey was conducted among 5 individuals to validate the need for this application.

**Survey Questions:**
1. Do you own more than 20 books or movies? (All 5 said Yes)
2. Have you ever accidentally bought a duplicate? (4 out of 5 said Yes)
3. Have you ever lost track of an item you lent to a friend? (5 out of 5 said Yes)
4. Would you use an app to track your collection? (All 5 said Yes)

**Conclusion:**
There is a clear demand for an application that helps users catalog their media, track lending, and avoid duplicate purchases.
