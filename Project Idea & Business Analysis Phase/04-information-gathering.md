# Information Gathering

To understand the requirements for this application, the following methods were used:

1. **Observation:** Personal observation of how individuals manage their bookshelves and movie collections at home.
2. **Informal Interviews:** Conversations with friends and family who own more than 50 books or movies to understand their pain points.
3. **Market Research:** Review of existing solutions (e.g., Goodreads for books, Letterboxd for movies) and identifying what features are missing (specifically, unified cataloging and lending tracking).
4. **Personal Experience:** Drawing from personal experience of buying duplicate books and losing track of lent items.
