# Stakeholder Analysis

**Primary Stakeholder (End Users):**
- **General Public:** Individuals who own books and movies and want to organize them.
- **Students:** University students who buy multiple textbooks and want to track them.
- **Media Enthusiasts:** People who enjoy rating and reviewing their favorite books/films.

**Secondary Stakeholders:**
- **Friends/Family:** People who borrow items from the primary user (indirect users).
- **Developers:** The solo developer building and maintaining the application.

**User Needs:**
- Easy to add and edit items.
- Quick search and filter capabilities.
- Clear status tracking (e.g., Reading, Watched, Loaned).
