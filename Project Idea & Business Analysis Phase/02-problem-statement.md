# Problem Statement

Many individuals own a significant number of books and movies but struggle to keep track of them. Common problems include:

1. **Forgetting Ownership:** People often forget which books or movies they already own, leading to accidental duplicate purchases.
2. **Lending Chaos:** Physical copies of books are frequently lent to friends, and the owner often forgets who borrowed them or when they were due back.
3. **Unwatched/Unread Piles:** Users buy movies to watch or books to read but lose track of their progress.
4. **Lack of Organization:** There is no centralized, digital place to store details (ratings, reviews, formats) about personal media.

Current solutions like simple notes or spreadsheets lack proper search, filtering, and status-tracking features. This app aims to solve these issues with a user-friendly web interface.
