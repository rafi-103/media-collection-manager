# Interviews

**Interviewee 1:** Sarah (Student)
- **Pain Point:** "I lend my textbooks to juniors and completely forget who has them by the end of the semester."
- **Requirement:** A lending tracker with friend names and dates.

**Interviewee 2:** John (Office Worker)
- **Pain Point:** "I buy movies on Blu-ray and later buy the digital version because I forget I already own the physical copy."
- **Requirement:** A searchable library to check if he already owns a specific movie.

**Interviewee 3:** Emily (Book Club Member)
- **Pain Point:** "I read 20 books a year but can't remember my ratings or reviews for book club recommendations."
- **Requirement:** A personal rating and review system.
